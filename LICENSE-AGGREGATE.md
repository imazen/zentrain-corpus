# Aggregate licensing

This corpus aggregates content under multiple licenses. Per-image
licensing is recorded in `manifest.tsv` (`license` column). Subfolders
under `mlp-tune/` carry the upstream LICENSE file when one was provided.

The most restrictive license in the corpus is **CC-BY-SA 4.0** (CID22).
Any picker, model, or derivative work trained on the full corpus
inherits the share-alike obligation: redistribute under CC-BY-SA 4.0
with attribution to the sources listed below. If you train only on a
subset that excludes CC-BY-SA material (e.g. just `gb82-photo` +
`gb82-screen` + `synthetic`), the resulting work can be released under
CC0.

## Sources and attribution

### `cid22-train/` — CID22 (CC-BY-SA 4.0)

Aaltonen, J., Mäenpää, M., Häkkinen, J., Eerola, T., Kämäräinen, J.-K.,
*"CID22: A Comprehensive Image Database for Image Quality Research"*
(2022). Provided by the Tampere University vision group.

Upstream LICENSE preserved at `mlp-tune/cid22-train/LICENSE`.

### `clic-train/`, `clic-1024-train/` — CLIC 2025 / Unsplash

CLIC 2025 challenge dataset, originally sourced from Unsplash. Unsplash
license: https://unsplash.com/license — free for commercial and
non-commercial use; attribution appreciated but not required.

`clic-1024-train/` contains center-cropped 1024×1024 Lanczos resamples
of the same source images, generated by
`~/work/codec-corpus/generate-clic2025-1024.sh` (vipsthumbnail +
smartcrop + sRGB tagging). Filenames preserved from upstream.

### `kadid10k/` — KADID-10k (Pixabay)

Lin, H., Hosu, V., Saupe, D., *"KADID-10k: A Large-scale Artificially
Distorted IQA Database"*, QoMEX 2019. Reference images from Pixabay
under the Pixabay Content License.

### `gb82-photo/`, `gb82-screen/` — Garry's Bench 82 (CC0)

Photographic and screen-content reference set curated by
imazen. Released under CC0 1.0 Universal — no rights reserved.

### `size-dense-renders/` — Lanczos3 multi-size renders (CC-BY-SA 4.0)

20 representative source images drawn from `cid22-train` and
`clic-1024-train`, each Lanczos3-resized to a log-spaced size grid
(32, 40, 48, 64, 80, 96, 128, 160, 192, 256, 320, 384, 512, 640, 768,
1024, 1280, 1536 px on the long edge). Used to give the picker enough
density along the size axis to fit `feat_min_dim` / `feat_pixel_count`
slopes. Inherits CC-BY-SA 4.0 from CID22 sources (clic-derived
variants would be Unsplash-licensed but are mixed into the same group;
attribute to both).

### `synthetic/` — Procedurally generated (CC0)

Gradients, checkerboards, uniform/Gaussian noise, hue sweeps, RGB
stripes, thin lines, and solids. Generated by
`tools/render_synthetic.py` with fully seeded RNGs. CC0 — no rights
reserved.
